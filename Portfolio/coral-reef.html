Coral Reef Cafe

An interactive underwater environment


Development: Unity 6


Art: Autodesk Maya

Coral Reef Cafe is a stylized and lighthearted narration on the relationship between humanity and marine life. What would the world look like underwater if it merged with our world in the future? It is meant to appeal to the viewer's understanding of oceanic aesthetics, then introduce more and more unexpected objects, creatures, and structures. As one of Téa's first interactive 3D environemnts, it represent an amalgamation of intial organic studies and large structures.

Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering.
