Missed Calls

An interactive mini-game to uncover the story of a missing girl


Development: Unreal Engine 5, Unreal Blueprints


Art: Autodesk Maya

Missed Calls explores the story behind an unknown character, brought from the perspective of a player in the midst of searching a case of a missing person. Through clues hidden around the small living space, the player may uncover more explicit details regarding the whereabouts of the homeowner. The game emphasizes experiences in exploring pieces of evidence, sound, and text. The developement of this story emphasized best practices for game feel and UI design, or the overall intersection between two-dimensional visuals, sound, and programming.
