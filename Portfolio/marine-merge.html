Marine Merge

An evolution game created for the Mini Jame Gam #43 on Itch


Development: Unreal Engine 5, Unreal Blueprints


Art: Autodesk Maya, Blender

Marine Merge was created as a puzzle game for the theme, "Overflow," with a special item "brush". On a team of two over the course of three days, Téa and her partner created an experience inspired by the classic evolution game in which the player cares for their aquarium and avoids overpopulating their tank with bigger and bigger aquatic creatures. The player may evolve from a tiny minnow to a great white shark if they play carefully and intentionally, learning the patterns of which fish evolve. While played on a 2D landscape, the visuals were inspired by a cartoon-shaded 3D structure. Téa's contrubution emphasized gameplay programming and 3D modeling.
