About

Téa Cassidy Held


Téa's work explores narrative and functional topics in the new developmental age of technology, with an emphasis in the cross section between new technology and art practices. She has a focus in creating virtual worlds which may develop a relationship with users in the form of interactive games or visual experiences. These projects, built from emerging tools and softwares, aim to suggest new ways of learning and experiencing our metaphysical world through innovative experiences.
