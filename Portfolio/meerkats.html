Meerkats

A realistic 3D environment

Development: Unreal Engine 5


Art: Autodesk Maya, Adobe Substance Painter, Procreate

Meerkats is a study of the realistic development of a modern, urban, indoor environment. It emphasized best practices for developing hard-surface models with computationally sound topology, as well as strong compositional aesthetics through the use of texturing and staging. The most influential practices involved in the creation of this environment includes modeling, UV unwrapping, texture painting, post-process effects, rendering, and project management. Narratively, Meetkats explores the night after a high-school dance. It is up to the viewer to decipher echoes of worldbuilding through the nature of the objects, textures, and mood.
