Subway Gounds

An indoor urban environment encompassing the world of robots and their relationship with human-made structures


Development: Unity 6


Art: Autodesk Maya, Blender, Adobe Substance Painter

Subway Grounds is an exploration of building a narrative world through modeling, staging, and texturing. The reality of humanoid robots is supported by the modern architecture of the space around them, the colorful nature of surrounding objects and lighting, as well as hidden images and textures scattered throughout the world.


Subway Grounds was additionally developed for a VR Youtube 360 Experience, in which a user may look in all directions and experience an audio and visual environment as the camera navigates seamlessly through the scene.
