Digital Clouds

A reactive, dreamlike environment


Development: Unity 6


Art: Autodesk Maya

Digital Clouds is an exploration of graphical environments as a means to express a feeling or mood. The inspiration for an unexpected collection of things comes from the global relationship between organic life and technology and our  relationship with old and new technologies, while also sharing a space with recognizable nature. The collection of seemingly random objects is meant to cause initial confusion and wonder, in which the theme may be uncovered with further exploration. Meshes with a low number of polygons were created to complimemt the abstract feeling of the scene.
