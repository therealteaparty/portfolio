Cozy Camp

A 4-directional tile based survival game


Development: Construct3, Adobe Audition


Art: Procreate

Cozy Campwas created to engage the player in the effects of both sound and art. All interactions are carefully matched with edited sounds, and each sprite has been developed with a created style, color palette, and meaning for the game mechanics and story. The scene takes place in an icy, snowy winter forest, in which you must hunt for materials to build a fire before nightfall. The final version of this game was created through intense playtesting from mentors and peers.
