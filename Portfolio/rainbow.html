Rainbow Cat Space Shooter

A classic 4-directional space shooter


Development: Godot 4, GDScript


Art: Procreate

Rainbow Space Shooter is the first game Téa has ever created with a graphical interface, sound, and working mechanics with user input. It is a quick and simple classic space shooter with a comedic twist using a personal artistic style. Develoment of this game emphasized learning clean and strong computational foundations for loops, data strcutures, and functions.




Luddy School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering.
