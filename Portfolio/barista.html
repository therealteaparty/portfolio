Milky Way Cafe

A cosmic barista simulator


Development: Unreal Engine 5, Unreal Blueprints


Art: Autodesk Maya

Milky Way Cafe is meant to provide a light-hearted game experience to busy the player with mundane tasks in a visually interesting environment. Playing on pastel colors and a cartoon shaded space, the first ever appearance of originally created android characters are introduced. The game's mechanics are based on a randomized ordering system combined with a physics handle used to interact with items.
